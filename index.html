Cyber Invaders

Defend against cyber threats across multiple levels!

How to Play

Cyber Invaders is a game where you defend against various cyber threats across six challenging levels.

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to move your character.
  • Press the space bar to fire at incoming threats.
  • Collect power-ups (represented by plasters) to gain the ability to fire three shots at once. These power-ups represent software patches and updates.<
  • Do not let the enermies reach you! If they do it is game over!
  • Progress through six levels, each featuring different cyber threats:
    1. Level 1: Phishing Emails
    2. Level 2: Viruses
    3. Level 3: Spyware
    4. Level 4: Malware
    5. Level 5: Ransomware
    6. Level 6: Face the Master Hacker
  • Defeat all threats to advance to the next level.
  • On the final level, face off against the Master Hacker to win the game!

Good luck, cyber defender!
